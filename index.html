Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 Eggs
  • 50g Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions:

1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.

2. Fry the pancetta and garlic in a pan.

3. Beat the eggs and mix with Parmesan cheese.

4. Combine everything and season with salt and pepper.
